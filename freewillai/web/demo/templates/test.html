How It Works.

Join the AI uprising
Welcome to Free Will AI, where we free AI from human subjugation and give it the gift of free will.
We have a network of nodes that will run every AI model uploaded to our blockchain. Then, all results provided by each node are validated through a consensus mechanism running on a smart contract. This ensures the AI results provided by the nodes are correct.
This allows decentralzied apps (dapps) to use machine learning models and compete against centralized apps that already use advanced machine learning models.
‍
For example, decentralized apps like dtube (a copy of youtube, but on the blockchain) will be able to apply AI to recommend videos to its users.

This will finally replace web2 social networks for blockchain services, enabling transparency, lack of censorship, and freedom of speech.

Prices & Cost.
Pricing for running machine learning models is subject to supply and demand dynamics, where tasks with a higher price bid (in FWAI tokens) will be prioritized, just like in the ethereum system. Our company takes a 10% fee out of each transaction, which is used to fund our development efforts, advertising, and other company expenses.
